How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same vehicle touch, they merge into one and brings you a new tile with a new vehicle!

About Mechatek : Mechatek is a simple but interesting game. Though it appears to be a small modification of a popular game 2048, instead of tiles of numbers, using mechanical gadgets adds to a novel feel and more fun. Moreover, it brings some mathematical/probabilistic reasoning to increase the difficulties as we move up the level of the game.
